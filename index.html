About

Atelier Dante Time is an independent watch atelier focused on restoration, servicing, and custom assembly of mechanical timepieces — with respect for craftsmanship and detail.

Quick info

  • Mechanical watches & vintage restorations
  • Cleaning, lubrication, regulation
  • Custom builds (limited pieces)
  • Finishing & presentation details

Services

Servis a renovace mechanických hodinek, citlivá práce s vintage díly a zakázkové sestavy. (Galerii doplníme, až budeš mít fotky.)
