About

Atelier Dante Time is an independent watch atelier focused on restoration and custom assembly of mechanical timepieces. We respect heritage, craftsmanship and detail — creating timeless luxury pieces.

Craft • Heritage • Precision

What we do

We restore vintage mechanical watches, service movements and create custom builds with a focus on long-term reliability and premium presentation.

  • Complete disassembly, cleaning, lubrication, regulation
  • Dial / hands preservation and careful refurbishment
  • Crystal upgrades (including sapphire where applicable)
  • Custom builds and boutique presentation
Typical turnaround: 2–3 weeks (depending on parts availability and condition).
Values

Our standards

Every piece is treated as a collector’s object. The goal is not only beauty, but also stable performance and honest workmanship.

  • Transparent approach
  • Respect for originality
  • Quality control & testing
  • Premium packaging & documentation

FAQ

Most common questions.

How long does service take?

Usually 2–3 weeks depending on parts availability and the watch condition.

Do you keep original dial and hands?

Yes. We prioritize originality and only recommend changes if it improves reliability or safety.

Can you upgrade to sapphire crystal?

In many cases yes, depending on the case construction and compatibility.

Do you source parts?

Yes. We can help source original parts when possible. Availability varies by brand/model.
